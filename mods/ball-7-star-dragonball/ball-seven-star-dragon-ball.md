# Seven-Star Dragon Ball

Only 6 more to find!. This mod reskins the basic dodgeball to look like the Seven-Star Dragon Ball from Dragonball. 

![7-star-dragonball-preview](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/KnockoutCity-Pirate-Radio/PirateRadio-Mods/refs/heads/main/mods/ball-7-star-dragonball/ball-7-star-dragonball.png)

## What it changes

- `basic_ball_albedo` - Replaces the ball's color/albedo texture
- `basic_ball_normals` - Replaces the ball's normal map for proper lighting
